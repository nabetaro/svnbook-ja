Version Control with

Subversion

"So, you wanna know about Subversion, eh?"

This is the online home of Version Control with Subversion, a free book about Subversion, a new version control system designed to supplant CVS. This book is a work in progress, which will be published by O'Reilly and Associates in mid-2004.

In the meantime (and, well, even thereafter), this is a place to read HTML and PDF versions of the book. We'll do our best to keep the site up-to-date. As Subversion development continues, the product will continue to grow new features, and we plan to continue documenting those changes.

Errata

The book is mostly complete, but still lacking coverage on certain topics. Other topics need to be clarified. The authors have some running "to-do" lists (see the REVIEW and TODO files in the XML sources). Before emailing feedback about errors or missing information, please take a look at those lists. We may already be aware of the problem. Thanks!

Contributing

Please send any questions, comments, patches, gifts, praise, or errors to the Subversion development mailing list (dev@subversion.tigris.org — you need not be subscribed). Be sure to include the word [BOOK] in the subject line, and [BOOK PATCH] if submitting a patch. Please make your patches against the XML sources.
