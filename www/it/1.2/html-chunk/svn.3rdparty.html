Appendice C. Third Party Tools

Subversion's modular design (covered in la sezione chiamata «Layered Library Design») and the availability of language bindings (as described in la sezione chiamata «Using Languages Other than C and C++») make it a likely candidate for use as an extension or backend to other pieces of software. For a listing of many third-party tools that are using Subversion functionality under-the-hood, check out the Links page on the Subversion website (http://subversion.tigris.org/project_links.html).
